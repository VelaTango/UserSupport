Privacy Policy

VT PDF Voice Read — by VelaTango
Last updated: June 2026

VT PDF Voice Read is designed with your privacy as a priority. This page explains what happens to your data when you use the app.

What stays on your device

All PDF reading and text-to-speech functionality runs entirely locally on your device. The app does not require an account, login, or email address. We do not collect, store, or have access to your PDFs, reading activity, or any personal information.

Optional AI summary feature

If you choose to use the AI summary or AI discussion feature, selected text from your PDF is sent directly from your device to Google's Gemini API, using an API key that you provide and manage yourself. This connection is directly between your device and Google's servers — VelaTango does not see, store, or have access to this data at any point. Use of the Gemini API is subject to Google's own terms and privacy practices.

This feature is entirely optional. You can choose not to use it.

No third-party advertising or tracking

This app contains no advertising, analytics, or tracking technologies of any kind.

Children's privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children, since no data is collected by VelaTango in the first place.

Changes to this policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted on this page with an updated date above.
